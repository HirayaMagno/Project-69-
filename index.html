Campus Life

school gym

Was established in the 1900

The gym at Erie Day School is where preformances take place. Meetings for the students also take place in here. It is very wide, and students play games and have reccess in this wide, open gym.

school library

Was built in 1980

This place at Erie Day School is where kids can read and relax. They can have a quiet time in here. It is a nice, cozy spot to sit and read a book.

school computer lab

Was built in 1982

This place is where students can do work on the computers. It is often used for online activities and coding. It is cozy, and the tables are lined with computers.

school achievements

Cross Country Race Around Frontier Park
kids raced around frontier park

This was a cross country race. Kids grades 3-8 run in cross country, and they raced around the park. Everyone was very good and ran as fast as they could to the finish line.

Erie Day School Establishment
The Erie Day School Establishment

Erie Day School was built on October 1st, 1929. It was later improved even more throughout the years.

Erie Day School Lego League
Lego League Competition

The Lego League is where kids group up and build a robot. The best robot will win the competition. There is an obstacle course where the robots race in it.
